Project Overview

The objective of this project was to design a modern chemistry laboratory furniture system that provides a safe and efficient working environment for researchers. Every workstation was planned to improve workflow while maintaining proper storage, accessibility, durability and ease of maintenance.


The project included laboratory planning, modular workstation development, chemical storage cabinets, overhead storage, under-counter units and island workstations. The complete design considered manufacturing feasibility, sheet metal fabrication, ergonomic dimensions and installation requirements.

Design Process

01. Client Requirement Analysis

The project started by understanding laboratory workflow, research activities, storage requirements, equipment placement, future expansion possibilities and user safety requirements.

02. Site Measurement & Space Planning

Site dimensions were studied carefully to optimise movement, maintain proper working clearance and provide an efficient laboratory layout while complying with ergonomic standards.

03. Product Design

Every workstation, cabinet and storage module was designed using modular principles for easy manufacturing, installation and future maintenance.

04. Production Planning

The complete furniture system was prepared considering sheet metal fabrication, assembly sequence, cost optimisation and installation feasibility.

Laboratory Layout Planning

The laboratory layout was designed to create an efficient workflow between workstations, storage units and circulation areas while maintaining adequate working space for researchers.

Furniture Collection

Each furniture module was designed independently while maintaining a common design language across the laboratory.

Island Workstation



The island workstation provides researchers with a spacious collaborative working area. The design integrates storage, utilities and comfortable working height while ensuring maximum durability for laboratory use.

Wall Workstation



The wall workstation maximises usable laboratory space while keeping equipment and storage within easy reach. Its modular construction allows future modifications with minimal effort.

Corner Unit



The corner unit efficiently utilises otherwise unused space and provides additional work surface along with storage without interrupting laboratory workflow.

Chemical Storage Cabinet



Designed for the safe storage of laboratory chemicals, the cabinet provides organised storage with durable sheet metal construction and easy accessibility for users.

Overhead Storage Cabinet



Overhead cabinets maximise vertical storage without occupying valuable floor space. They provide quick access to frequently used laboratory items while keeping workstations clutter-free.
