UltraTech Corporate Office Interior & Furniture Design

Complete workspace planning, office furniture design, manufacturing drawings and production support for a modern corporate office environment.


Project Overview

This project involved the complete design and development of corporate office furniture for UltraTech. The scope included executive furniture, modular workstations, conference tables, PA room furniture and office space planning. Every product was designed considering ergonomics, aesthetics, manufacturing feasibility and installation efficiency.

Office Layout Planning

Developed the office layout by considering circulation, departmental requirements, meeting spaces, executive cabins, workstations and user comfort. The planning ensured optimum utilization of available space while maintaining efficient workflow.


Staff Workstation

Designed modular workstations to maximize productivity while providing sufficient storage, cable management and ergonomic seating arrangements.




Conference Table

A modern conference table designed for collaborative meetings, integrating functionality, aesthetics and ease of manufacturing. The design was developed with proper seating dimensions, cable management and durable construction.




Executive Desk

Designed an executive workstation with premium finishes, integrated storage and a contemporary appearance suitable for senior management cabins. The product was developed from concept through manufacturing documentation to final production.






PA Room Table

Designed a compact office table for the Personal Assistant area, ensuring efficient use of space while maintaining a consistent design language across the office environment.

Project Outcome

Successfully delivered a complete corporate office furniture solution including office planning, executive furniture, conference rooms, modular workstations and production-ready manufacturing drawings. The project combined engineering principles, modern aesthetics and manufacturing feasibility to ensure a smooth transition from concept design to installation.
